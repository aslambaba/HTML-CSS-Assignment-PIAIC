PIAIC

Presidential Initiative for Artificial Intelligence & Computing

Presidential Initiative for Artificial Intelligence & Computing (PIAIC) is a privatly funded not-for-profit educational Initiative started by president of Pakistan, Dr Arif Alvi, Kazi rehat ali, and Zia Khan to help promote education, research, business oppertunities in emerging tech fields like

  • Cloud Native Computing
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Blockchain
  • Internet of Things

Cloud Native Computing

The Could Native Computing program prepare the student to program in open source technologies like Linux, Docker, Kubernetes, JavaScript, Node.js, React and React Native while introducing the students to developent of Microservices and software development techniques on cloud platform like AWS.

Artificial Intelligence

The Artificial Intelligence program of PIAIC takes the student throught foundation of AI technology, via teaching programming in open-source-language like Python, Numpy, Pandas, Tensorflow 2.0, and Flask and teaching deploying AI solutions and use cases, and ending with real-life examples of modern day AI in practice.

Blockchain

The main emphasis of Blockchain program is to understand the blockchains like Ethereum and Hyperledger (Fabric), and learn to program in languages such as Solidity, JavaScript on frameworks like Node.js. The program also introduces them to deployement of Distrubuted Apps (DApps), Frameworks like Web3 and Truffle and an in-depth understanding of Blockchain-as-a-Service (BaaS) and how to develop and offer an Initial Coin Offering (ICO) of a crypto token.

Internet of Things:

The Internet of things program introduce the student to open source development technologies like Rust, Embedded Rust, WebAssembly, Hyper, Diesel, Google Assistant, and Kubernetes while making them efficent in developing Micro Services for IOT-Edge, IoT Cloud, and voice computing for device control.
